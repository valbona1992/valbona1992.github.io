Our Story

There's a kind of coffee shop that doesn't exist much in the West. The kind where you sit for hours, where the coffee is an excuse to stay, where no one is rushing you out the door. I grew up knowing that feeling — through Balkan coffee culture, through the way people gather, linger, and actually talk. That slowness wasn't laziness. It was intention.

Sabr is an Arabic word meaning patience. It traveled into the Albanian language through Islam, woven into our everyday speech, our prayers, our way of moving through hard things. I wanted to bring it back — not as something heavy or religious, but as an invitation. A reminder that slowing down is its own kind of strength.

Sabr & Sips was born from that. A space where the drink in your hand is just the beginning. Where you're allowed to take up time, take up space, and just be. No rush. No guilt. Just good drinks, good company, and a little patience with yourself.

Drink. Pause. Repeat.
